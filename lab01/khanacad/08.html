France is a top global travel destination, renowned for its, art, wine, and landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum. Ideal for year-round visits, with peak seasons in spring and summer, visitors can explore diverse regions from Paris to the French Riviera and Loire Valley. Key requirements include a valid passport for the Schengen area, and in 2026, a new ETIAS authorization for many visitors.
